Package name: p2_lizijun
Author: Zijun Li
Collaborator: none

Part 1:
- Uses turtlebot_navigation with AMCL and sends PoseStamped goals one at a time.
- Edit config/p2a_params.yaml to set your own map_file and L1/L2/L3 poses.

Part 2:
- Uses RGB and depth streams to detect a red ball, estimate distance, and control /cmd_vel.
- Default target distance is 1 meter.
